Explorer panes

Crossover pane

Crossover pane has the intention to help visualize binary operations. It expect objects of the same specie dropped into his parents slot and user click of operation button.


Mutation pane

Mutation pane, actually works only for lisp-math-expression language, which is a free of grammar rules language used sometimes for symbolic regression. Here a "color vector" language uses it and can be used to see how mutations affect at a specific point of the tree. When clicking a node, children populates with new individuals with a new copy of the parent individuals with a new subtree in selected node.


Buffer panes

This buffer is just used to store temporal copies of objects you want. Images and expression list views available.
